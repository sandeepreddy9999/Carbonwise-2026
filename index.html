🌎 What is a Carbon Footprint?

A carbon footprint is the total greenhouse gases generated by our actions. The global average is 4.8 tonnes per year (~13.5 kg/day). To limit warming to 1.5°C, we must cut emissions to under 2 tonnes per person by 2050.
